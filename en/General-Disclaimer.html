Fundamentals of Farmat

  1. Farmat is the pseudonym of the head of ICOFS.
  2. ICOFS is a community of people in technical fields, creativity and hobbies.
  3. ICOFS can act as a commercial or non-profit organization depending on the situation.
  4. ICOFS and Farmat are not one thing.
  5. Some personal Farmat accounts serve as official ICOFS accounts.
  6. Some official ICOFS accounts act as personal Farmat accounts.
  7. ICOFS invites those who truly want to create, explore, discover;
  8. No one can be excluded from ICOFS against their wishes.
  9. ICOFS adheres to the ideas of globalism. No one can be limited because of their origin.
  10. ICOFS writes in one of the official languages of the UN.
  11. ICOFS follows a formal style.
  12. ICOFS has the following accounts: https://icofs-info.github.io/pdf/doc1.pdf
